The Deadly Surgeon

Born into a humble family, Duckoo was the quiet kid in the pond. Because of his quiet nature, he was the target of bullies in his kindergarten school. He had to protect himself and that led him into boxing. His father bought him his very first boxing gloves on his third birthday.

But as a student, he excelled in all of the subjects taught in school.Duckoo won every prize in school throughout his young life and did the same in college where he won honors too for outstanding research. He was the Smartest Duck-tor that graduated from one of the world's top Medical school.
