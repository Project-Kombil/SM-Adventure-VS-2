Ducky The Software Engineer

Ducky was no ordinary duckling when he was born. He was born with a calculator! It was no ordinary calculator, it was the FX 2000 X92A D3A2 Model! The most expensive calculator of that age. This calculator was only used by the wizards of NASA when out exploring other planets for signs of life. But this duckling grew up learning the art of calculaton with the help of this this special calculator.

Designing his first Missile Homing Software at the age 2, Ducky was a well respected Engineer among all the engineers around the world. Because of his intelligence, internet giant Google hired him right before his second birthday. WIth his inventions and fame, he became very rich at a very young age.

Rumours have been going around that he even helped his Uncle Ducko in getting past the US borders through an old railway tunnel underground. He used his connections with the US government to grant access for his uncle to deal drugs within the big cities of the United States.
